How to Contact PC Matic Support

Get Support

Get Help for Your PC Matic Account

If you need assistance with your PC Matic account, there are several ways to receive help. Whether you're experiencing login problems, activation issues, installation errors, subscription concerns, or antivirus-related questions, the official support resources provide step-by-step guidance to resolve common issues.

Ways to Contact PC Matic Support

  • Submit an online support request through the official support portal.
  • Use the customer help center to browse troubleshooting articles.
  • Contact the billing department for subscription-related questions.
  • Request assistance for installation or activation issues.
  • Use password recovery if you cannot sign in to your account.
  • Review frequently asked questions before submitting a request.

Support Services

Customer support can help with installation, software updates, activation, login problems, billing inquiries, account recovery, subscription management, and troubleshooting.

Information to Prepare Before Contacting Support

  • Your registered email address.
  • Subscription or order details.
  • Your Windows version.
  • A detailed description of the issue.
  • Any error message displayed on your screen.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Use the password recovery option available on the login page to receive a reset email.


Why is my activation code not working?

Verify that your activation information matches your account and that your subscription is active.


Can I get help with installation?

Yes. Support resources provide installation instructions and troubleshooting steps for common setup issues.
